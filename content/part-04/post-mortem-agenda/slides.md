# Post-Mortem Agenda
*Roles and documentation standards*

---

## Agenda overview
- Timeline review
- Impact summary
- Root cause analysis
- Action items
- Follow-up dates

---

## Who attends and why
- Incident responders
- Service owners
- Facilitator
- Scribe
- Business stakeholder

---

## Documentation standards
- Central template
- Link tickets and commits
- Record lessons learned
- Add metrics like MTTR
- Share widely

---

## Key takeaway
Clear agendas and records keep post-mortems focused and actionable.
