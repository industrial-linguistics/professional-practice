Speaker 1: Tool selection checklist focuses attention on a concrete part of the work. Does the vendor meet your minimum security posture (SSO, audit logs, retention, regional hosting)?, Can it integrate with your identity, CRM, finance stack and data warehouse without brittle workarounds?, and What is the true cost at 2× headcount—base price, add-ons, overages and migration effort?.
Speaker 2: In practice, ask who owns the work, what evidence proves it happened, and what handoff comes next. Use the supporting details as a checklist: Can it integrate with your identity, CRM, finance stack and data warehouse without brittle workarounds?; What is the true cost at 2× headcount—base price, add-ons, overages and migration effort?; Is there a clear exit path—data export, contract terms, and knowledge transfer—if the tool stops fitting?.
